Tangsuyuk

Tangsuyuk, also known as Sweet and Sour Pork. The recipe is from Maangchi. The full recipe including a YouTube tutorial can be found here!

Tangsuyuk is the Korean variation of the Chinese sweet and sour pork dish. A protein of your choiceis fried and tossed in a delicious sweet and sour sauce. Although pork is the most common protein in this dish, chicken, beef, and tofu are wonderful substitutes!
